Based on the framework a online web editor is being developed for graphical editing.

About Erasmus for Entrepreneurs

The Erasmus for Young Entrepreneurs program will support you in starting your business, for example in becoming a freelance developer, and to develop relevant skills for managing and expanding their own business. You will be working together with a local host entrepreneur (so for example with layerJS). The duration of the stay abroad can be between one and six months. The European Union provides financial support to the new entrepreneurs for their stay abroad.

In order to become funded by the program you will need to provide a business plan describing the business you intend to start, for example a web development company. Please note: You are not required to eventually found that company if you don't want or can't. This is an opportunity to find out whether this could be an option for you. And it is even funded. No strings attached.

Once accepted you will be able to connect to host entrepreneurs from different EU countries. Of course we would be super happy if you choose to make your first entrepreneurial experience in our team. You can definitely get in touch with us before you start your Erasmus application and we may be able to support you with the application.
